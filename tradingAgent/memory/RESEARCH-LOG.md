# Research Log

Daily pre-market research entries appended here. Every buy must reference a
catalyst documented in that day's entry. Format each entry as follows:

```
## YYYY-MM-DD — Pre-market Research

### Account
- Equity: $X
- Cash: $X
- Buying power: $X
- daytrade_count: N

### Market Context
- WTI / Brent:
- S&P 500 futures:
- VIX:
- Today's catalysts:
- Earnings before open:
- Economic calendar:
- Sector momentum:

### Trade Ideas
1. TICKER — catalyst, entry $X, initial trailing stop 10%, target $X, R:R X:1
2. ...

### Risk Factors
- ...

### Decision
TRADE or HOLD (default HOLD if no clear edge)
```

Optional afternoon addendum may be appended from the midday scan if something
material happens intraday — under a `### YYYY-MM-DD — Midday Addendum` subheading.
